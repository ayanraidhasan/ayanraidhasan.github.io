Monty Hall Game

Rules
1. You are given the choice of three doors.
2. Behind one door is a car, behind the other two doors, are goats.
3. Choose one door initially.
4. The host (who knows what is behind each door) reveals to you one of the two other doors.
5. This revealed door always contains a goat.
6. You are given the option of sticking with your initial door or to switch to the other unopened door.
7. You either win or lose.
8. Is it more strategic to stay or switch?

Click on any door to play!
